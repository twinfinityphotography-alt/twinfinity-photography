Frequently Asked Questions

Do prices include travel?

No — travel and accommodation are billed separately based on location and logistics.

How do we receive photos and videos?

High-resolution images are delivered via online gallery and USB (on request). Video is delivered digitally; albums are delivered physically if selected.

How do I confirm a booking?

Confirm via WhatsApp and a 30% advance to secure your date. Balance is due upon delivery.

How many images do we get?

Every event is different, but you can expect a thoughtfully curated selection that tells the complete story — typically 250–600+ photos for a full-day event.

Can you match a specific editing style?

We are happy to align on mood and tone. Our signature look is elegant, true-to-life color with refined highlights and rich shadows.
